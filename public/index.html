Simulation assumptions and known inaccuracies: Enemies, incoming damage, and crowd control are not simulated. Operator attack ranges are not modeled; placement on the grid is used only for adjacency checks. Projectile travel time and attack windup animations are not simulated. Surtr's S3 HP drain is modeled as a discrete event every 6 ticks. Haruka's Talent 2 bubble heals are excluded. Warfarin's SP gain from enemy deaths is excluded. Mon3tr's bounce chain is resolved within the same tick. Nearl's S1 is enabled automatically when adjacent to Surtr on the grid. Nearl and Vendela's healing-received passives apply multiplicatively when deployed. Reconstruction aura (+15% ATK) is auto-applied to operators within its surrounding 8 tiles.
